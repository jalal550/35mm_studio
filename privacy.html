Privacy & Policy

Welcome to 35mm Studio

At 35mm Studio, we value the trust and privacy of our clients. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you engage with our services.

1.Information We Collect

Contact Information: When you engage with our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address to communicate with you and provide our services effectively.

Editing Resources: We may collect and store resources provided by you for the purpose of editing, such as raw footage, images, audio files, and other media assets. These resources are used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your editing requests and are treated with strict confidentiality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Client Communication: We use your contact information to communicate with you regarding project updates, inquiries, and feedback. This includes sending project proposals, discussing project requirements, and providing delivery notifications.

Editing Services: Your editing resources are used exclusively for the purpose of fulfilling your editing requests. Our team of professionals utilizes these resources to create high-quality, customized video content according to your specifications.

3.Data Security and Confidentiality

Secure Handling: We take extensive measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your information. Our systems are protected with industry-standard security protocols to safeguard against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

Confidentiality: We respect the confidentiality of your editing resources and personal information. Your data is accessed only by authorized personnel directly involved in the editing process, and we do not share or disclose your information to third parties without your consent.

4.Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control: You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to modify or remove your data from our records, please contact us, and we will promptly assist you.

Opt-out: You may choose to opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by contacting us or unsubscribing from our mailing list. However, please note that opting out of communications may affect our ability to provide you with timely updates or information about our services.

5. Updates to this Privacy Policy

Policy Revisions: We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for any updates. Your continued use of our services after changes are made indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
